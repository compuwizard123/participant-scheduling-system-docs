\section{Executive Summary}
This document's purpose is to detail the participant scheduling system proposed by the \index{Human-Computer Interaction Lab} Human-Computer Interaction Lab of Wisconsin-Madison. It is the third document describing this project, and specifies requirements not captured by use cases, as well as a detailed user interface.  The project exists because the lab wishes to unify their schedule information and provide a simple, intuitive interface for prospective participants to sign up for experiments.
